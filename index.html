Attom

Attom was an attendance tracking system designed to register and monitor the signing in and out of sixth form students. The app was fully implemented at the saltash.net community school sixth form for year 12 and 13, before being replaced by a fingerprint-based system in 2017. It used NFC cards which were given to users and then swiped against an Android tablet which used a native Android app to process the swipe and output a custom audio and visual message to the user.

Users of the app commented on its ease of use and its speed compared to the old paper system, with almost 98% of students rating it good or outstanding as a system.

PRZE (formerly Zappr)

PRZE is a peer-to-peer and traditional praise system for the SECRET skills. The system was used by year 7 students as part of their integrated learning curriculum and was also displayed to both Hillcrest Christian College (Queensland, AUS) and SIVECO Romania, both giving positive reviews of the software. Currently, a native Android app is in the late stages of development, along with a web platform, all designed (including graphics), developed and tested by myself. An Ubuntu server was set up by myself and run through appropriate CDNs to ensure security and speed.

The school commented on several occasions about the quick turn around time of developments and professionalism throughout the planning, design, testing and evaluation phases of development.
